All the Crystals Game

A random number is generated at the start of the game.

Each of the four "crystals" below represents a random number and clicking on them you will add a specific amount of points to your to the total score.

Win game by matching your total score to the random number. Lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
